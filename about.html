Our Story

Jeph Logistics was founded in 2021 with a simple vision: to provide the people of Kete-Krachi with a reliable, efficient, and affordable delivery and transportation service they can truly depend on.

Starting with a single motorbike and a passionate dream, we have grown into a trusted name in logistics and delivery across the community. Every package carries trust, urgency, and care — and that is why we treat every delivery as if it were our own.

Our Mission

To make delivery and transportation simple, stress-free, and affordable for everyone in our town. We bridge the gap between people, businesses, and opportunities by ensuring goods and services move seamlessly to where they’re needed most.

Our Vision

To become the leading local delivery and transportation company in the Oti Region and beyond, known for speed, professionalism, and unmatched customer satisfaction.

Our Core Values

  • Reliability: We always deliver on time.
  • Integrity: Every package is handled with honesty and care.
  • Customer First: Your needs drive everything we do.
  • Innovation: We adopt modern approaches to make delivery easier and faster.
  • Community: We exist to serve and uplift our local community.

Our Impact

Beyond just delivery, we are deeply committed to supporting the growth of local businesses in Kete-Krachi. By providing fast and affordable logistics, we enable restaurants, shops, and entrepreneurs to reach more customers and grow their businesses. We also create jobs for young people passionate about service and hard work.

Looking Ahead

Jeph Logistics is not stopping here. Our goal is to integrate advanced tracking systems, mobile applications, and a wider transportation fleet to serve customers even better. We envision a future where no delivery request is too small or too big.
